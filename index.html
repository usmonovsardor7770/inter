FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Where is the event taking place?

Event is taking place at Millburn High School in Cafeteria.

Can I bring my friends to the Event?

Yes you can, but you have to tells us about it while you are registering to the event. If you already registered and then decided to bring your friend, just go the register page and make sure you are signed into same account as you first registered to the event and from there you can edit your response.

How long will the event last?

International Night will last for 2 hours and it will start at 7pm and end at 9pm. Event will take place at Friday, May 19th.
