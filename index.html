International Family Night

Event is on Friday, June 1st, 2018

Register Now

International Family Night is the event for the students and parents of the students to learn about other cultures. It is created by ELL students for people in seeking their curiosity to learn about others cultures. The event includes ELL students traditional foods to be tried by others and learn about their country.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where is the event taking place?

The event is taking place at Millburn High School in Cafeteria..

Can I bring my friends to the Event?

Yes, you can, but you have to tells us about it while you are registering for the event. If you already registered and then decided to bring your friend, just go the register page and make sure you are signed into the same account as you first registered to the event and from there you can edit your response.

How long will the event last?

International Family Night will last for 2 hours and it will start at 7 pm and end at 9 pm. The event will take place on Friday, June 1st.
